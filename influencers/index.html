Frequently Asked Questions

What audience size do I need to get accepted?

We work with creators starting from 1,000 engaged followers. We care more about audience engagement and alignment than raw numbers. If your audience includes travel creators or aspiring influencers, you're likely a good fit.

How much can I realistically earn per month?

Earnings vary based on which MUOB tier your referrals choose and your commission level. Partners typically earn £150-600 per month, with top performers reaching £1,000+. Explorers (£9) gives smaller commissions but easier conversions, while First Class (£39) gives higher commissions per referral.

When and how do I get paid?

Monthly payouts via PayPal or bank transfer on the 1st of each month. Minimum payout is just £20, so you can start earning quickly. All payments are tracked transparently in your dashboard.

What marketing support do you provide?

Complete marketing toolkit including professionally designed graphics, video assets, email templates, and content guidelines. Growth and Elite partners get custom assets created specifically for their brand.

Can I promote across all my social platforms?

Absolutely! Use your referral link across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, email newsletters, and any other platforms. We provide platform-specific content and strategies for maximum conversion.

How quickly can I start earning?

Most approved partners start earning within their first week. After approval, you'll receive your materials and unique link immediately. The faster you start promoting, the faster you start earning.
