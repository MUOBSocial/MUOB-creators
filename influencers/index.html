Frequently Asked Questions

What's the minimum follower requirement?

We look at engagement over follower count, but typically accept influencers with 1,000+ engaged followers. Quality matters more than quantity.

How do commission surges work?

When you consistently drive conversions and create great content, we increase your commission rate. Top performers can earn 75%+ commission on all sales.

When do I get paid?

Commissions are paid monthly via PayPal or bank transfer. You'll receive payment on the 1st of each month for the previous month's earnings.

How do private hotel stays work?

Based on your performance and audience, we'll invite you to complimentary stays at partner hotels. You'll create content for MUOB during these trips.

Can I promote on multiple platforms?

Yes! The more platforms you use, the better. We provide assets optimized for Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and more.
