Who I Am

I'm a tech content creator and product designer based in San Francisco. I've been reviewing productivity tools and tech gear for five years — helping people build better workflows and make smarter buying decisions.

I started making content because I was tired of overhyped reviews that didn't answer real questions. My goal: create honest, useful content that actually helps people.

Productivity App Launch
AUGUST_2024

Created a 5-video tutorial series for a new task management app. Content covered initial setup, advanced features, and workflow integrations with other productivity tools.

Videos were designed to be educational but engaging — showing real use cases instead of just feature lists. Filmed in my actual workspace using the app in my daily routine.

Result: Videos generated 300K+ views across YouTube and TikTok. Content contributed to 2,000+ app downloads in the first month and a 4.7% conversion rate from video views.

Desk Accessory Brand Collaboration
SEPTEMBER_2024

Filmed an aesthetic desk setup video featuring cable management solutions and monitor arms. Content focused on both the visual appeal and functional benefits of the products.

Used smooth camera movements and close-up product shots to showcase build quality and design details. Added voiceover explaining why each product improves my workflow.

Result: Video was used in Instagram ads and organic posts. Drove $12K in attributed sales within two weeks and became the brand's top-performing creator content.

Content Approach

Clean. Minimal. Informative. I film with natural light and focus on product details — texture, functionality, and real-world use. My videos are easy to follow, visually satisfying, and built to perform across platforms. I prioritize clarity over hype because that's what actually drives purchases.
