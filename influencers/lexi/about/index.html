My Story

I started creating skincare content in 2021 after struggling with acne and trying everything. What began as documenting my own skin journey turned into a full-time career creating UGC for the brands I genuinely use.

I'm a combination skin girly with a minimalist routine philosophy. I don't gatekeep, and I don't fake results. That authenticity translates into content that performs.

Case Studies
Indie Skincare Brand Launch
AUGUST 2024

Created 12 UGC videos for a new vitamin C serum launch. Content included unboxings, application demos, and "real results" check-ins over 30 days. Videos were used in Meta ads targeting women 25-40.

Result: Videos drove over $47K in attributed sales with a 4.2% conversion rate. Client extended partnership for additional product launches.

Clean Beauty Subscription Box
SEPTEMBER 2024

Developed a 3-video series for a monthly skincare subscription service. Focus was on showcasing product variety and the unboxing experience. Content was designed to feel personal and trustworthy.

Result: Content resulted in 800+ new subscriptions in the first two weeks and a 3.8% click-through rate on paid ads.

My Content Style

Natural lighting. Close-up shots. Real skin (texture and all). I film in my bathroom or bedroom with my phone because that's where people actually use skincare. My videos feel like a friend showing you what worked for them — because that's what they are.
