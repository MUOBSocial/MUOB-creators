Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a big following to join?

No! We work with creators of all sizes. Quality content and engagement matter more than follower count. Many successful creators in our community started with under 1,000 followers.

Will I get hotel stays immediately?

Timeline varies based on your location, content quality, and application success rate. Most active creators secure their first collaboration within 30-60 days of joining.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your membership at any time. There are no long-term contracts or commitments required.

What type of content do hotels want?

Hotels typically look for authentic, high-quality content that showcases their property, amenities, and guest experience. This includes photos, videos, reels, stories, and testimonials that can be used in their marketing.
