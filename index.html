Frequently Asked Questions

How many hotel briefs do you post?

We post new briefs every Friday. The number varies but typically 3-5 opportunities per week, depending on season and hotel partnerships.

Do I need a certain number of followers?

No minimum required. Hotels value quality content and engagement. We have members with 500 followers successfully landing collaborations.

Are hotel approvals guaranteed?

No. Hotels make their own selection decisions. We provide the opportunities and tools to improve your chances, but success depends on your content quality and pitch.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts or commitments. Cancel directly from your account dashboard. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

What's the difference between tiers?

Higher tiers get earlier access to briefs (First Class on Wednesday, Nomads on Thursday, Explorers on Friday), better hotel perks, paid opportunities, and more support.
